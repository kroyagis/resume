Parallel Play / Video Sculpture

  • Created a video sculpture which questions the nature of causality through the presence of three parallel videos
  • As the videos play in and out of synch with each other, the sculpture creates a random moment of meaningful coincidence.
  • The sculpture consists of CRT televisions and DVD players. Chroma keying and stop motion animation were used for the videos.
  • Selected as one of the 15 out of 32 art pieces to showcase at graduation showcase
